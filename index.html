The LIVEXTRA World

LIVEXTRA sits between fashion label, creator universe, and music-driven visual culture.

After-dark energy

Everything feels nocturnal, charged, and slightly dangerous. The mood is more blackout function than polished campaign.

Visual-first presence

The brand hits through motion first: edits, reels, shorts, clips, and visuals that land before people even hear the explanation.

Streetwear attitude

Clean silhouettes, hard graphics, and logo-led pieces built to feel uniform, not merch.

Creator-led motion

LIVEXTRA moves like a living project. New visuals, new sounds, and new pieces keep feeding the same identity.
